About Me

WHERE ART MEETS TECHNICAL PRECISION

I am a 3D Artist specializing in 3D modeling, texturing, and rendering. My main focus is creating realistic product visuals and detailed hard-surface models using Blender and Substance Painter.

I enjoy turning ideas into clean, professional 3D visuals that are both creative and technically strong. I am always looking to grow, explore new opportunities, and create better work with every project.
